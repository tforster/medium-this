Settings

Medium This requires access to your Medium account. This is done via a revokable token that you control.

After you have logged in to your Medium account go to your settings page and scroll down to the section "Integration tokens". Enter a description such as "Medium This", click "Get integration token" and paste a copy of the new token below.

You can revoke the token at any time on your settings page and this extension will be immediately deactivated.
